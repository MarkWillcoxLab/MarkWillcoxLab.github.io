Research Facilities

State-of-the-art equipment and specialized laboratories

Characterization Laboratory

Advanced microscopy, spectroscopy, and analytical equipment for materials characterization. Includes scanning electron microscopy, atomic force microscopy, and various spectroscopic techniques.

  • Scanning Electron Microscopy
  • Atomic Force Microscopy
  • Spectroscopic Analysis

Synthesis Laboratory

Chemical synthesis facilities for nanomaterials and advanced materials preparation. Equipped with fume hoods, analytical balances, and specialized synthesis equipment.

  • Chemical Synthesis
  • Nanomaterial Preparation
  • Safety Equipment

Device Fabrication

Clean room facilities for device fabrication and testing of energy materials. Includes photolithography, thin film deposition, and device testing equipment.

  • Clean Room Facilities
  • Device Testing
  • Thin Film Deposition
