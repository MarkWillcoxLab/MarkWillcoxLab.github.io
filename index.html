Welcome to Our Research Group

The Mark Willcox Research Group is a medical microbiology lab dedicated to pioneering research in antimicrobial development, focusing on novel antibiotics and disinfectants. Our interdisciplinary approach combines microbiology, chemistry, and clinical applications to address the global challenge of antimicrobial resistance.

Lab Goals

Our mission and objectives in advancing antimicrobial research

Primary Objective

Develop novel antimicrobial compounds and antibiotics to combat resistant bacterial infections and emerging pathogens that threaten global health security.

Prevention Focus

Advance next-generation disinfectants and sanitization technologies for healthcare, industrial, and public health applications to prevent infections.

Education & Training

Train the next generation of researchers and healthcare professionals in antimicrobial development and resistance management strategies.

Research Excellence

Maintain the highest standards of scientific rigor and innovation in all our research projects, ensuring reproducible and impactful results that advance the field.

Collaboration

Foster strong partnerships with academic institutions, healthcare facilities, and industry partners to accelerate the translation of research findings into clinical applications.
